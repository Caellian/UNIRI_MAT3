\subsection{Tangenta i normala grafa}

\textbf{Tangenta} na graf funkcije $f$ u to훾ki $(x_0,y_0)$ ima jednad탑bu:

\begin{gather*}
t\dots\quad y-y_0=f'(x_0)(x-x_0).\\
f'(x_0)=0\implies t\dots\quad y = y_0
\end{gather*}

\noindent
\textbf{Normala} na graf funkcije $f$ u to훾ki $(x_0,y_0)$ ima jednad탑bu:

\begin{gather*}
n\dots\quad y-y_0=\frac{1}{f'(x_0)}(x-x_0).\\
f'(x_0)=0\implies n\dots\quad x = x_0
\end{gather*}
